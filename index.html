The Seven Genocides Assignments

Per assignment instructions, we were to pick one of the listed genocides and conduct research on what occurred. The genocide that I have chosen to study is the Armenian Genocide. I first heard about the Armenian Genocide from a friend who told me that his grandparents worked in the embassay at the time. When the genocide, his grandparents had to hide in a sewer for three days before being able to escape to Egypt. Unfortunately, this was the case with many others and nearly 2 million people were not lucky enough to escape at all. As a person that comes from minority group that has suffered from religious persecuation for centuries, I felt obligated to research and learn as much about the Armenian Genocide as possible and present it to you today.

In this assignment we will explore the events that occurred during the Genocide, and address the following questions:

  • What are the long-term effects of the Armenian genocide for the country or people involved?
  • What are the overarching lessons that can be learned from studying the Armenian genocide?
  • How did the Armenian genocide connect to "The Ten Stages of Genocide" by Dr. Gregory H. Stanton?
  • Why is the study of genocide important, and how can it be intellectually enlightening?
  • What are the personal challenges and difficulties that one may encounter in studying genocide?
  • How can genocides be avoided and combated in the future?
